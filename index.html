The book

An annual reference, built to be kept

Irish whiskey has grown from a handful of working distilleries to more than fifty in little over a decade, and it has no annual record. BONDED is the first reference written to mark each year in Irish whiskey as it happens, meticulously researched and verified distillery by distillery.

It serves two readers at once. The visitor who has just finished a distillery tour and wants to dive deeper into the category, and the enthusiast who already knows their pot still from their malt and grain. Each section goes deeper than the last, so both find their way in.

The inaugural focus

The year of Single Pot Still

The first edition takes Irish single pot still whiskey as its subject. The style that is Ireland's own, and the one now at the heart of a proposed amendment to the Irish Whiskey Technical File.
